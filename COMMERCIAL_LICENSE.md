## Who Qualifies as the Sponsor

For these terms, the "sponsor" is the legal person or legal entity that is the payer of the active GitHub Sponsors subscription.

- Individual sponsorship: rights are granted only to that individual person.
- Organization sponsorship: rights are granted to that legal entity.
- Team or managed-account payments: rights are granted to the legal entity that owns or pays for the sponsor account.

Unless separately agreed in writing, sponsor rights do not automatically extend to:

- parent companies, subsidiaries, or sister companies
- contractors or clients of the sponsor

If an organization needs group-wide usage rights across multiple legal entities, contact the licensor for a separate commercial agreement.

- The commercial grant remains valid only while sponsorship is active at or above USD 1 per month.
- A grace period of 15 calendar days applies after a failed or canceled payment.
- If sponsorship is not restored within the grace period, the commercial grant terminates automatically.

After termination of sponsorship-based commercial rights:

- continued use is permitted only under AGPL-3.0-or-later

Eligibility is determined using the sponsor status visible to the licensor through GitHub Sponsors records.
